Arnold Schwarzenegger

  • Seven-time Mr. Olympia winner
  • Four-time Mr. Universe winner
  • 1969 World Amateur Bodybuilding Champion

Highlights

Arnold Schwarzenegger at his prime. Mr. Olympia 1975. Where he won his sixth consecutive Mr. Olympia title.

Pumping Iron (1997). American docudrama about the world of professional bodybuilding, with a focus on the 1975 IFBB Mr. Universe and 1975 Mr. Olympia competitions.

The Terminator is credited with solidifying Schwarzenegger's status as a leading man.

Governor of California (Nov 2003 to Jan 2007).
